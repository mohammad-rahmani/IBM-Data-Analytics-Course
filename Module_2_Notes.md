# Module 2 Summary: The Role and Skills of a Data Analyst

## 1. Core Responsibilities of a Data Analyst
* **Data Acquisition:** Gathering and acquiring data that best serves the specific business use case.
* **Data Preparation & Analysis:** Cleaning and analyzing data to understand what it truly represents.
* **Interpretation & Communication:** Interpreting results and effectively communicating key insights to stakeholders.
* **Documentation:** Ensuring the analytics process is fully documented for future reference and repeatability.

---

## 2. Essential Skill Set for Data Analysts

### A. Technical Skills
* Proficiency in spreadsheets and statistical tools.
* Experience with data visualization tools.
* Knowledge of programming and querying languages (e.g., Python, SQL).
* Ability to work with various data repositories and big data platforms.

### B. Functional Skills
* Solid understanding of statistics and analytical techniques.
* Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to view situations from multiple perspectives.
* Data visualization and project management capabilities.

### C. Soft Skills
* **Collaboration & Communication:** Ability to work in teams and communicate findings clearly.
* **Data Storytelling:** Telling a compelling story with data to gain stakeholder buy-in.
* **Traits:** Curiosity to explore new pathways and intuition developed from historical data experiences.
